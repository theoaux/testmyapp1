About Us

Believers' LoveWorld Campus Ministry is the youth arm of Believers' LoveWorld, a unique and dynamic ministry called by God to play a chief role in the end time move of the Spirit. Our Vision is to carry God's divine presence to all the people of the world, demonstrating in them the character of the Spirit. The President of the Believer's LoveWorld Inc. , Pastor Chris Oyakhilome, the host of the popular TV healing/miracle program " Atmosphere for Miracles " and his wife Pastor Anita Oyakhilome, have a passion to see lives transformed by the power of God. Through various outreach crusades,magazines, audio and video tapes and books including the best selling daily devotional " Rhapsody of Realities ", they have brought the reality of the divine life to many. Millions of young people across the globe have been affected by this ministry, many of whom have dedicated their lives to reaching other young people in the world.

Testimonies

Being in believers loveworld has been a wonderful experience. I always had the desire to share the gospel with others and lead many to Christ.This dream was not realized until I joined BLW campus ministry in August 2010. After the 2012 ICLWC, I was challenged by the new standards given to us by our Man of God, Rev Chris. I made up my mind not to have a cell membership strength less than twenty-five.I spent time to speak in tongues, envisioned how my cell would be and I spoke words forth by the spirit .I also took time to review the cell ministry manual and other pastors messages concerning soul winning and the cell system. I started to reach out to souls from room to room in my catchment area. People responded to me like never before and many of them gave their lives to Christ and got filled with the Holy Ghost. They joined the cell and fellowshiped with us well. It was so inspiring seeing them win other people, growing in their giving and functioning in various departments in the church. I was so fulfilled to see these souls now totally transformed and my cell membership strength kept on increasing. I pioneered a total of 6 new cells within one year. Now I am a senior cell leader. Praise God!!The word works!!!
