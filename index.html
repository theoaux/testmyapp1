About Us

Believers' LoveWorld Campus Ministry is the youth arm of Believers' LoveWorld, a unique and dynamic ministry called by God to play a chief role in the end time move of the Spirit. Our Vision is to carry God's divine presence to all the people of the world, demonstrating in them the character of the Spirit. The President of the Believer's LoveWorld Inc. , Pastor Chris Oyakhilome, the host of the popular TV healing/miracle program " Atmosphere for Miracles " and his wife Pastor Anita Oyakhilome, have a passion to see lives transformed by the power of God. Through various outreach crusades,magazines, audio and video tapes and books including the best selling daily devotional " Rhapsody of Realities ", they have brought the reality of the divine life to many. Millions of young people across the globe have been affected by this ministry, many of whom have dedicated their lives to reaching other young people in the world.
